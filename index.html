Sara is a storyteller who inspires and encourages women to see themselves in a different light.

Through Golden Hour, Sara uses her 15+ year portrait photography experience and convictions of her Catholic faith to frame life in an authentic and personal way.

With aspects of photography as her metaphor, she radiates interior joy, captures connection, and leaves each room a little brighter than she found it.
